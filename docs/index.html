Outer Product as an Introduction to APL and a Pretty Cool Thing in General

My workshop at LambdaConf 2019. Video (2 hr 25 min).

The main repository contains these slides as well as other materials including Dyalog APL code for you to run on your own.

Start at the first slide, or see all slides on one page. When viewing a slide navigate with the arrow keys or their equivalents wasd: left and right to move to adjacent slides, down to advance animation, and up to reset animation. You can also advance animation by clicking anywhere inside a slide. Skip entire sections using , and . or q and e.

There is also a page with commentary on the slides, which I recommend reading alongside the talk or after viewing it.

Slides are designed for 1920x1080 resolution, and the DejaVu font family.
